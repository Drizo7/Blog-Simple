COLLEGE RELATED STRESS


The Stressful Parts Now...

A little stress is good; it motivates students to overcome challenges. However too much stress can have a negative impact on a student. College is an environment that has different people of different backgrounds and therefore everyone can have stress. When I first got into college, the stress that bothered me and still bothers me is academic stress; not wanting to be weeded.

Completing coursework such as assignments and studying , preparing for the mid semester and end of semester exams. Combining those activities and multiply by the time you should attend each classes every day not forgetting time to chill makes it stressful. You can see that not all academic stress comes from the lecturers but also having poor time management too. There's so much other stress we students face that can have negative impact on our studies.
